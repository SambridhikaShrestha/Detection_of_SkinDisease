Welcome to Skin Disease Detection

Skin Disease Detection is a website that helps you identify skin diseases and provides information on how to treat them.

How it works

To use our skin disease detection feature, simply upload an image of the affected area and our advanced algorithm will analyze it to determine the most likely skin disease.

We also provide a detection log where you can keep track of your past detections for reference or to share with your healthcare provider.
